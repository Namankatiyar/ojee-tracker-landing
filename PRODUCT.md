# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Developers, power users, and teams looking to track metrics or data efficiently via a dashboard interface.

## Product Purpose
A clean, high-performance landing page to showcase the dashboard tracker tool. Success is measured by users opening the tracker directly (Primary CTA) or checking out the GitHub repository (Secondary CTA).

## Brand Personality
Sleek, developer-focused, high-performance, and dark-mode first.

## Anti-references
- Overly generic cream/beige warm neutral SaaS templates (AI slop feel).
- Cluttered, low-contrast dashboard layouts with too many nested cards.
- Distracting, slow animations that gate information.

## Design Principles
1. **Showcase the Interface**: Put the visual language of the tracker dashboard front and center.
2. **Path of Least Friction**: Ensure the primary action ("Get Started" to open the tracker) is highly visible and instantaneous.
3. **Developer-First Craft**: Lean into high-precision borders, clean typography, and snappy Framer Motion interactions to build trust with a technical audience.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG AA color contrast ratios for dark mode.
- Keyboard accessible navigation and interactive elements.
- Motion respect (reduce/disable animations under `prefers-reduced-motion`).
